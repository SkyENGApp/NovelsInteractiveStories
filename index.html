Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection and Usage

Dracula: King of Darkness is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identity data (such as names, phone numbers, or email addresses) from our players. Your narrative choices and game progress are stored locally on your device.

2. Advertising and Tracking

To support the development of this game, we display third-party advertisements. These ad partners may use device identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA) to provide personalized advertisements and analyze ad performance. You can manage your tracking preferences through your device settings or the App Tracking Transparency prompt upon launch.

3. Third-Party Services

Aside from the advertising partners mentioned above, we do not share any data with third-party analytics or cloud services. We do not maintain any user profiles on external servers.

4. Contact and Support

If you contact our support team via email, we use your contact information solely to resolve your issue. We do not sell or use this information for marketing purposes. Contact us at: codelovin@outlook.com.
